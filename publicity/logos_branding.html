Logos and Branding

Name

The theatre is named and should be advertised as The Nottingham New Theatre.

Logo

Print Screen

The NNT logo is avalible in full colour, white and black variants. The latter two variants should be only used when the full colour version would not work (e.g. on top of an image).

The logos should only be scaled and not edited. The minimum recommended width for print is 65mm.

EPS Files

These files are fully scaleable and transparency works nicely. You will need Photoshop (or compatable software) to work with them however.

  • Full colour: NT_Full_Colour_Logo.eps
  • Black: NT_Black_Logo.eps
  • White: NT_White_Logo.eps

Fonts
