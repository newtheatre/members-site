Elements of Set

  • Platform- Used to elevate the acting area or to put seats on it. Platforms of various sizes are available to rent from Hawthorns. The Theatre has Prolytle Stage Dex platforms that are 1mx2m and 1mx1 in size with legs to make final platform height at 20cm, 40cm, 60cm, or 80cm. Additional platforms and legs can be hired from College Street rob.kettridge@collegest.org.uk (Nottingham City Council). Legs are the same diameter as scaffolding poles which can be hired in from a variety of sources. Steel deck 4'x8' in size (and others) are available to hire from Hawthorn Theatrical http://hire.hawthorns.uk.com/catalogue/category.aspx?id=1101 and OnEvent Production Compnay http://www.lovingitlive.co.uk/hire.php.
  • Wagon- same construction has a platform but has wheels (casters).
  • Flat- Used to create walls or some other background. The Theatre has blank flats, flats with window frames, and flats with door frames in them.
  • Window
  • Door
  • Softgoods -curtains & drops. Used to create an image (eg cityscape, landscape), or conceal parts of the set or Theatre

If using any flammable materials, including (but not limited to) paper, cardboard, (externally purchased) fabrics, you will need to use flame-proof spray called flamebar (pronounced 'flam-bar'). You will need to budget for this, and can find it in various quantities online.

Paint

The Auditorium stage and wings are painted black with Flints. You can buy it in 5L tins, and are around £20+VAT+delivery per tin. It takes around 75% of one tin to get an even coat on the stage floor, and approximately a full tin if you include the wings.

The stage will be painted at the beginning of every In-House season, and it is the responsibility of shows to purchase paint and repaint it black should it get painted for the show.
