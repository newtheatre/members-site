Soundcraft Si Expression 2

This is a digital sound desk, it has 24 recallable mic pre amps plus 4 line inputs, 4 internal stereo FX returns. For more information on the default setup of the desk in the theatre see the sound plans.

Training

Learning how to use the sound desk is part of basic sound training. For more information contact the Technical Director.
