Strand 300

The theatre's old lighting desk. In September 2013 the ETC Ion look over in the main space.

An old but still very good lighting desk. It's licensed for 50 fixtures with 50 attributes, and has two playbacks along with 24 faders.

Capable of tracking, and has four pages of 24 subs.

strand_300_quick_start.pdf A two page 'quickstart' that highlights the main functions of the desk
