Tech At The Nottingham New Theatre

The Tech Crew plays an integral role in the running of shows at the theatre. For the most up to date information about what's happening this week please join the tech Facebook group here

Being part of the tech team is a great way to get involved with the Nottingham New Theatre. Every show relies on the creativity and competency of every tecnical crew member in order to fulfill the director's vision and produce a slick, clean show. There is no need for you to have any prior experience with anything technical, we can provide all the training and support you could ever need. To that end, there will be training during each semester to get everyone started.

Tech gives you an opportunity to learn new skills and be creative as well. You get to liaise with the director, the actors and the set building team in order to create a spectacular production and have a lot of fun along the way.

Your contact for getting involed with tech is the Company Technical Director (Luwa Adebanjo) (techdirector@newtheatre.org.uk). Please do not hesitate to ask any questions at all and please do get involved!

These pages serve as a home for useful resources and documentaion about the equiptment we own and the way we do things. If there are any other resources you would find useful, contact the Company Tecnical Director.
