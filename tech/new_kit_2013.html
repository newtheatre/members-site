New Kit for 2013

As you may know several generous donations have been made by a small group of alumni to improve the technical aspects of the theatre. The major/interesting bits of new kit is listed below.

This is a very exciting time for tech in the theatre, there will be much to do at the start of term and plenty of training to be done. See you then!

Lighting

Fixtures

  • 12x Selecon Acclaim 650W Fresnel
  • 4x Selecon Rama 1200W Fresnel
  • 6x ETC Source 4 Junior Zoom Profile 575Watt
  • 10x PAR16 Birdies

A broader range of lighting fixtures will open up new opportunities for lighting design.

Console

We've purchased an etc_ion console with a 20 fader wing. This is a much more modern lighting console and the logical step up from our existing Strand 300.

If you fancy getting some knowlege of the console before you're back in the theatre there are some (long) but in depth videos that will help you out.

Accessories

50 sheets of colour, restocking our already broad selection. http://gels.newtheatre.org.uk has been updated.

Small number of breakup and window gobos.

Single channel dimmer for side wall house lights.

Sound

An entirely new PA is to be installed in the main space consisting of two tops and a sub. Read more on the sound pages. Our old system was poor and sounded bad, the new system will be awesome.

Sound Desk

A Soundcraft Si Expression 2 has been purchased. This is a digital desk (over our current analogue one), and is quite a step up. We've also acquired a 12 channel multicore to run to stage, this gives more flexibility in terms of audio inputs.

QLab

A QLab licence, along with a Mac Mini have been purchased. This is a reliable computer based sound playback system. Using a cue stack, it gives you a GO button like a lighting desk. QLab is the industry standard in this regard for running sound for live performance.

In conjunction with a sound card donated half way through last year we will have 8 mono outputs from QLab, this is great flexability.

Comms and Show Relay

Two new beltpacks have been purchased.

A new show relay system, covering the majority of backstage areas is to be installed (this is not fully confirmed yet).
